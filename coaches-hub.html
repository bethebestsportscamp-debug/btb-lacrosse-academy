Teaching

Player Learning Modules

Short training modules used to teach BTB players core concepts.

Module 1

Riding Principles

Why we ride, how we ride, and what every player must know about transition defense. Covers press ride, zone ride, and soft ride philosophies, plus individual responsibilities by position.

Module 2

Clearing Responsibilities

Position-by-position clearing duties. Goalie outlets, midfield lanes, attack positioning. How to move the ball from our defensive end to the offensive end under pressure.

Module 3

Defensive Transition

Getting back, matching up on the run, and communication systems. The first 5 seconds after a turnover determine whether you get scored on or lock down.

Module 4

Communication Systems

Ball, help, fire, slide — the BTB defensive vocabulary every player must master. Clear, consistent communication is the backbone of every great defense.
