About the Tournament

Where Elite Teams Compete

The BTB World Series brings together the most competitive youth lacrosse programs for a weekend of high-level play, college exposure, and championship-caliber competition.

Elite Competition

Top-tier teams from across the region compete in age-specific brackets designed to challenge every roster and elevate the level of play.

Exposure

College coaches, scouts, and recruiters attend the World Series to evaluate top talent. Put your players on the national stage.

Championship Culture

From opening ceremonies to the final whistle, every detail is built to deliver a championship-level experience for players and families.

Tournament Format

Schedule & Format

A structured three-day format designed to crown a true champion through pool play, bracket seeding, and single-elimination playoffs.

01

Pool Play

Round-robin games to establish bracket seeding and ensure every team plays multiple games.

02

Bracket Play

Single-elimination brackets based on pool play results. Win or go home.

03

Championship

Division finals on championship Sunday under the lights. Trophies, MVP awards, and bragging rights.
