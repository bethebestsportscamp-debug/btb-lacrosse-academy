Who We Are
About BTB Lacrosse

Our Mission

Develop complete lacrosse players through elite coaching, innovative technology, and championship-level training systems.

What Makes Us Different

BTB is the first AI-powered lacrosse academy. Interactive playbooks, real-time analytics, searchable drill libraries with video, digital coaching binders, and game-day sideline systems.

Our Philosophy

Player development over wins. Lacrosse IQ over athleticism. Every player gets better every season. Boys and girls programs run identical coaching systems with position-specific curriculum.
